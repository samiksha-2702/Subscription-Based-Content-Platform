About PrepEdge

PrepEdge is an AI-powered interview preparation platform designed to help students and professionals build strong technical and communication skills through structured learning, real practice tests, and intelligent feedback systems.

Platform Overview

PrepEdge is a smart learning ecosystem that combines artificial intelligence with structured educational content. It analyzes user performance, identifies weak areas, and provides a personalized learning path to improve interview readiness efficiently.

Problem We Solve

Most students face difficulty in interview preparation due to lack of structured content, proper feedback, and real-world practice. PrepEdge solves this by providing guided preparation instead of random learning.

Key Features

AI Learning System
Personalized recommendations based on user performance.

Mock Tests
Real exam-like environment for practice and evaluation.

Performance Analytics
Tracks accuracy, speed, and improvement areas.

Interview Preparation
Company-wise questions and coding challenges.

Skill Tracking
Identifies weak topics and suggests improvements.

Smart Dashboard
Clean analytics view of your preparation journey.

Our Vision

To become a complete AI-driven career preparation platform that helps students confidently transition from learning to employment with structured guidance and real-world preparation tools.
