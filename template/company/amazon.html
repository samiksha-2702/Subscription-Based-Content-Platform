Interview & Selection Process

Interview Rounds

  • Online coding assessment (OA)
  • Technical Phone Screen (1-2 rounds)
  • On-site/Virtual Loop (4-5 interviews)
  • Leadership Principles interview (2-3 rounds)
  • Bar Raiser round (final)
  • System Design (for SDE-2+)

Preparation Focus

  • Data Structures & Algorithms (LeetCode Medium/Hard)
  • Low-level & High-level System Design
  • STAR format for behavioral questions
  • Amazon Leadership Principles (memorize all 14)
  • Practice with mock interviews
  • Review Amazon-specific coding problems
Prep Tip: Practice coding on LeetCode (Amazon tagged questions) and prepare 2-3 STAR-format examples for each Leadership Principle. The Bar Raiser round focuses on culture fit and high standards.

Leadership Principles

Amazon evaluates every candidate against these 14 principles. Prepare STAR stories for each.

Customer Obsession Ownership Invent & Simplify Are Right, A Lot Learn & Be Curious Hire & Develop Best Insist on Highest Standards Think Big Bias for Action Frugality Earn Trust Dive Deep Have Backbone Deliver Results
STAR Method Required Prepare 30+ stories
