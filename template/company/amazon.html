Company Overview

Amazon is a global leader in e-commerce, cloud computing, and AI-driven technology solutions. Founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon has expanded from an online bookstore to a technology powerhouse delivering innovative products worldwide.

  • Founded: 1994
  • Headquarters: Seattle, Washington, USA
  • CEO: Andy Jassy
  • Mission: To be Earth's most customer-centric company.

Job & Internship Opportunities

Amazon hires for software, cloud computing, data science, logistics, and product management roles.

  • Software Development Engineer Internships
  • Data Science & AI Internships
  • Cloud & AWS Internship Programs
  • Full-time roles in AWS, Alexa, Prime, and Supply Chain
  • Official Careers Page: amazon.jobs

Technologies & Tools

  • Languages: Java, Python, C++, JavaScript

  • AI/ML: Alexa AI, AWS AI services
