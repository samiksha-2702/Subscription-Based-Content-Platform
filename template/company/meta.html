Interview & Selection Process

Interview Rounds

  • Online coding assessment (1-2 problems)
  • Technical Phone Screen (45 min)
  • On-site/Virtual Loop (4-5 interviews)
  • Coding + DSA interviews (2-3 rounds)
  • System Design (for E4/E5+)
  • Behavioral & Leadership interviews

Preparation Focus

  • Data Structures & Algorithms (LeetCode Medium)
  • System Design (scale, performance)
  • Product sense & technical communication
  • Move Fast culture alignment
  • React/GraphQL knowledge (for frontend)
  • Practice with Meta-tagged questions
Prep Tip: Meta values speed and efficiency. Practice solving LeetCode Medium problems in under 20 minutes. Be prepared to optimize solutions and discuss trade-offs.

Meta's Core Values

Move Fast Be Bold Focus on Impact Build Social Value Be Open Create Positive Family
