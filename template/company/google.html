Company Overview

Google LLC is a global technology leader known for its search engine, AI research, cloud computing, and innovative products. Founded in 1998 and headquartered in Mountain View, California.

  • Founded: 1998
  • Headquarters: Mountain View, California, USA
  • CEO: Sundar Pichai
  • Mission: Organize the world’s information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Job & Internship Opportunities

  • Software Engineer Internships
  • Product Management Internships
  • AI / ML Research Internships
  • Full-time roles in Cloud, Android, YouTube, and Search
  • Official Careers Page: careers.google.com

Technologies & Tools

  • Languages: Python, Java, C++, Go
  • Frameworks: TensorFlow, Angular, Flutter
  • Cloud: Google Cloud Platform (GCP)
  • AI & ML: Google AI and DeepMind
  • Data Tools: BigQuery, Dataflow

Interview & Selection Process

  • Online coding challenge
  • Technical interviews (DSA + System Design)
  • Behavioral / HR interviews
  • Focus on analytical thinking and problem solving
