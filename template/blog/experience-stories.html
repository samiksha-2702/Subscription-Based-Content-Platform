Common Challenges

  • Time management during coding rounds - running out of time
  • Behavioral interview preparation - not having enough stories
  • System design understanding for senior roles
  • Nervousness and anxiety affecting performance
  • Communicating thought process clearly
  • Handling rejection and staying motivated

Key Lessons Learned

  • Start preparation at least 3-6 months in advance
  • Mock interviews are as important as coding practice
  • Learn from peer experiences and mistakes
  • Don't ignore behavioral rounds - they matter 50%
  • Quality over quantity in problem solving

Advice from Candidate

  • Show willingness to learn
  • Practice coding out loud

  • Be collaborative, not aggressive

Top Tips from Successful Candidates

  • Start preparation early (6 months before target)
  • Focus on problem-solving, not just memorization
  • Take mock interviews seriously - use interviewing.io
  • Analyze mistakes regularly and maintain an error log
  • Research company culture and values deeply
  • Network with employees for referrals and insights
  • Practice explaining solutions out loud
  • Don't ignore behavioral - prepare STAR stories

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not practicing with a timer
  • Jumping into coding without clarifying requirements
  • Not testing edge cases
  • Poor communication during problem solving
  • Not asking clarifying questions
  • Giving up too easily on hard problems
  • Neglecting system design for experienced roles
  • Not following up after interview
Insider Note: Most rejections happen not because of coding mistakes, but because of poor communication or not fitting company culture.
