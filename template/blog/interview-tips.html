Introduction

Cracking interviews at top tech companies requires strong technical skills, problem-solving ability, and effective communication.

Coding Interview Tips

  • Practice problems on LeetCode, HackerRank, and Codeforces.
  • Focus on data structures like arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, and hash tables.
  • Master algorithms such as recursion, dynamic programming, BFS/DFS, greedy, and divide & conquer.
  • Clarify the problem before coding and consider edge cases.
  • Write clean code with clear variable names.
  • Explain your thought process during interviews.

System Design Interview Tips

  • Learn scalability, load balancing, caching, and database concepts.
  • Practice designing systems like URL shorteners, chat apps, and social media feeds.
  • Use diagrams to explain architecture clearly.
  • Discuss trade-offs between performance and reliability.

Behavioral / HR Interview Tips

  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Prepare answers for questions like "Tell me about yourself".
  • Show teamwork and leadership experiences.
  • Maintain confident body language and clear communication.

Mock Interviews & Practice

  • Take mock interviews with peers or mentors.
  • Use interviewing.io for practice.
  • Review experiences on Glassdoor.
  • Track progress and revisit weak areas.

Suggested Daily Preparation Plan

  • 1–2 hours solving coding problems.
  • 30 minutes revising system design concepts.
  • 30 minutes behavioral practice.
  • Weekly mock interview.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not practicing consistently.
  • Ignoring time complexity.
  • Rushing into coding without planning.
  • Giving vague HR answers.
  • Not researching the company.
