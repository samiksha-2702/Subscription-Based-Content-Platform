Salary Trends (US - 2025)

  • Entry Level (0-2 years): $90k - $130k
  • Mid Level (3-5 years): $130k - $180k
  • Senior Level (6+ years): $180k - $250k+
  • Staff/Principal: $250k - $400k+
Remote Work Impact: Remote and hybrid roles now account for 35% of tech jobs, with similar compensation to in-office roles.
