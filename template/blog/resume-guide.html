Create a professional resume that stands out to recruiters

Introduction

A well-crafted resume is your first step toward landing your dream job.

Resume Structure

  • Header: Full name, contact information, LinkedIn/GitHub links.
  • Professional Summary: 2-3 lines summarizing your skills.
  • Education: Degrees and universities.
  • Skills: Technical and soft skills.
  • Experience: Jobs, internships or projects.
  • Certifications: Certificates and awards.

Resume Writing Tips

  • Keep resume 1 page for students.
  • Use action verbs.
  • Quantify achievements.
  • Use clean formatting.
  • Tailor resume to job description.
  • Use ATS keywords.

Common Mistakes

  • Long paragraphs.
  • Irrelevant skills.
  • Generic descriptions.
  • Poor formatting.
