Interview Skills

Prepare effectively for HR and technical interviews

Overview

Interview skills are essential for securing job opportunities. Strong interview performance reflects confidence, communication ability, subject knowledge, and professional attitude. Proper preparation significantly increases the chances of selection.

Types of Interviews

  • HR Interview
  • Technical Interview
  • Panel Interview
  • Group Discussion
  • Behavioral Interview
  • Online/Virtual Interview

Before the Interview

  • Research the company thoroughly
  • Understand the job description
  • Prepare common HR questions
  • Revise core technical subjects
  • Prepare your resume explanation
  • Choose formal and neat attire

During the Interview

  • Maintain proper posture and eye contact
  • Listen carefully before answering
  • Speak clearly and confidently
  • Give structured answers
  • Be honest if you do not know an answer
  • Avoid interrupting the interviewer

Common HR Questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why should we hire you?
  • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Why do you want to join our company?

After the Interview

  • Thank the interviewer politely
  • Send a follow-up email if appropriate
  • Note areas for improvement
  • Continue preparation for next opportunities
