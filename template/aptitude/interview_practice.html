🤝 Interview Practice

1. Tell me about yourself?

Tip: Summarize your background, skills, and career goals concisely.

2. What are your strengths and weaknesses?

Tip: Be honest. Mention strengths aligned with the job and weaknesses you are improving.

3. Why do you want to work for this company?

Tip: Show you researched the company and are aligned with its vision.

4. Explain a challenging project you worked on.

Tip: Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) to structure your answer.

5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Tip: Focus on growth, learning, and contribution to the organization.
